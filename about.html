About Mindanao

Mindanao is a big island and its land area is as big as the combined area of Hokkaido and Shikoku. Known as the "Land of Promise," it is rich in biodiversity and natural resources, with fertile soil and rare typhoon occurrences, offering immense potential in agriculture.

Visitors can explore numerous islands and beaches in the southern part of the archipelago. Besides white-sand beaches, Mindanao boasts some of the highest and most scenic waterfalls in the Philippines. Start planning your trip to Mindanao!
